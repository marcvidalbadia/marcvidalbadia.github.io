Marc Vidal

Postdoctoral Associate at the Department of Data Analysis, Ghent University. I completed my PhD/MSc in Mathematical and Applied Statistics at the University of Granada and PhD in Art Science at Ghent University. I develop statistical methods and algorithms for preprocessing and analyzing data from neurophysiological monitoring techniques (EEG, MEG, MRI, pupillometry), combining elements of infinite-dimensional and high-dimensional data analysis. My primary emphasis is on data-driven modeling and inference of neurotransmitter activity during states of motor and emotional arousal associated with embodiment processes.
