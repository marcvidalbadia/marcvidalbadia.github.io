Marc Vidal

PhD/MSc in Mathematical and Applied Statistics · PhD in Art Science

Research Associate, Ghent University   •   Guest researcher, Max Planck Institute for Human Cognitive and Brain Sciences

I develop statistical methods and algorithms for preprocessing and analyzing data from neurophysiological monitoring techniques (e.g., EEG, MEG, MRI, pupillometry), integrating elements of infinite- and high-dimensional data analysis. My interests include: Functional and high-dimensional data · Dimensionality reduction (ICA and Factor Models) · Neuroimaging preprocessing and statistics · Neural dynamics
